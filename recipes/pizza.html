Pizza

Make your own version of this classic dish that will rival any pizza palor

Intredients

  • Pizza Dough from Trader Joes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella recommended)
  • Toppings of choice (fresh basil recommended)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°
  2. Flour a pizza pan or baking sheet and roll out the dough to desired thickness
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1/2" gap near the edges for the crust
  4. Top with cheese, basil, and other desired toppings
  5. Cook in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown
  6. Enjoy!
