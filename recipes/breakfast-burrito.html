Breakfast Burrito

Literally the best breakfast food ever invented. You will be making this nonstop for days!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • 2 eggs
  • Hash browns (1/2 cup)
  • 3 strips of bacon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Shredded Cheese (multi-cheese blend is recommended)

Instructions

  1. Add a generous helping of vegetable oil to a frying pan
  2. Add hashbrowns and sprinkle salt on top
  3. Fry hashbrowns on medium heat until crispy brown on bottom (~5min). Flip and repeat on the opposite side
  4. On a second pan, fry bacon on medium-low heat until crispy on bottom (~4min). Flip and repeat on the opposite side
  5. In a bowl, scramble the eggs and add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Once the hashbrowns or bacon is done (whatever happens first), pour the scrambled eggs into the pan and fry until no longer runny
  7. Cover the tortilla in a wet paper towl and microwave for 20 seconds
  8. Cut the bacon into tiny pieces
  9. Add the hashbrowns, eggs, and bacon to the tortilla and sprinkle with cheese
  10. Wrap everything in the tortilla and microwave for another 20 seconds
  11. Enjoy!
