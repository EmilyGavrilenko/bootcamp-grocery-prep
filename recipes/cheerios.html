Cheerios

It may be basic, but Cheerios are a tried and true way to start any morning with a satisfied stomach

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Honey Cheerios

Instructions

  1. Pour the Cheerios into a bowl (IMPORTANT: The cereal goes before the milk!!!)
  2. Pour the milk over the cereal (amount may vary person to person)
  3. Enjoy!
