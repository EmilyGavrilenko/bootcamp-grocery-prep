Salad

A quick and easy healthy snack

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Walnuts
  • Parmesian Cheese

Instructions

  1. Chop up the lettuce into small, bite-size pieces
  2. Place chopped up lettuce into a bowl
  3. Top lettuce with walnuts and cheese
  4. Pour balsamic vinegar over the salad to taste
  5. Enjoy!
